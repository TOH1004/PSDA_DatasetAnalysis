## 📖 Project Overview

Sleep plays an important role in maintaining both **physical and mental health**. Poor sleep quality or irregular sleep habits can lead to various health issues such as:

- Weakened immunity
- Heart disease
- Anxiety and depression
- Weight gain

This project investigates how **sleep habits relate to health and lifestyle factors** using statistical data analysis techniques.

The analysis is conducted using the **Health and Sleep Relation Dataset**, which contains **374 samples** describing sleep behavior, health conditions, and lifestyle indicators.

---

## 📂 Dataset

Dataset Source: **Kaggle – Health and Sleep Relation Dataset**

The dataset includes information such as:

- Sleep duration
- Quality of sleep
- Stress level
- Physical activity
- Health conditions
- Lifestyle factors

These variables allow statistical analysis of how sleep affects overall health.

---

## 📊 Statistical Methods Used

Several statistical techniques were applied in this project:

### 1️⃣ One-Sample Hypothesis Test
Used to determine whether a sample mean significantly differs from a population mean.

### 2️⃣ Correlation Analysis
Measures the strength and direction of relationships between variables.

### 3️⃣ Regression Analysis
Examines how independent variables influence sleep-related outcomes.

### 4️⃣ Chi-Square Test of Independence
Tests whether two categorical variables are related.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to apply statistical analysis techniques to understand the relationship between **sleep behavior, lifestyle habits, and health conditions**.

---

## 📌 Conclusion

Through hypothesis testing, correlation, regression, and chi-square analysis, the project provides insights into how lifestyle and health variables are associated with sleep quality and duration.

Understanding these relationships helps highlight the importance of **healthy sleep habits for overall well-being**.
